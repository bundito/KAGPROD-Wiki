===== Folder and File Hierarchy =====

All projects are stored at the KAG Productions folder of the fileserver. 

{{:hierarchy-files-folders.png?400|}}

Within each project, the structure may vary depending on the client, project, or when it was worked on. Consult Kristen or use your best judgment when working in these directories.

When [[transfer|transferring data from a USB drive]], copy directly into the KAG Productions folder. The USB drives contain the hierarchy we're used to from those projects, so we're just making an exact duplicate.
